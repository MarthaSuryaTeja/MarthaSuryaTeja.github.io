About

The propeller pendulum is a system used to study the stability and control of dynamic systems. It consists of a pendulum with a motor and propeller that produces thrust to control the motion of the pendulum. How it works:

  • Sensors track the pendulum's current angle,
  • A controller adjusts motor speed based on the difference,
  • Goal: keep the pendulum balanced (making an unstable system stable).
