About

The Attendance System is an innovative and streamlined solution designed to automate the process of tracking student attendance and time spent in a lab or other designated spaces. The system uses a barcode scanner to capture each student’s registration number, which is linked to their personal information stored in a database. The system retrieves and displays key details such as the student's name, registration number, and image when the barcode is scanned. Additionally, it records whether the student is marking an entry or exit, and calculates the total time spent in the lab. The system operates with an intuitive Graphical User Interface (GUI) created on TinkerCAD, which allows users to easily interact with the system. Once the student scans their barcode, the GUI updates in real time, showing their status (entry or exit) and the amount of time spent inside the lab. All this data is then automatically logged in an Excel spreadsheet, where it is organized for easy tracking and analysis. The spreadsheet includes the student’s name, registration number, status, and timestamps for entry and exit, as well as the total time spent in the lab.
