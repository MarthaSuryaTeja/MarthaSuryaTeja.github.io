About

The Vishishtah Project is an advanced initiative focused on developing a semi-humanoid robot with autonomous capabilities, cutting-edge sensor technology, and human-robot interaction features. Designed to perform a wide variety of tasks, Vishishtah integrates LIDAR technology for precise navigation, enabling it to map its surroundings with high accuracy and make autonomous decisions in real time. This allows the robot to move efficiently through complex environments, avoid obstacles, and adapt to dynamic changes in its surroundings. The LIDAR system enhances its ability to navigate safely, whether in indoor spaces like homes and offices or in more challenging outdoor environments, giving it a significant advantage in autonomous mobility.
